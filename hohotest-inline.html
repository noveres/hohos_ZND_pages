梨花酥 6入 Hana Blossom Pineapple Cake

職人手藝揉入台灣土鳳梨，搭配鳳梨花乾，酸甜馥郁、果香明亮，品嚐島嶼熱帶風土的優雅綻放

Crafted with Taiwanese native pineapple and adorned with pineapple blossom. Tangy and aromatic, with a bright fruitiness - a refined expression of the island's tropical terroir.
